Some Versions of Megatron

IDW

Origin: Miner/writter founder of the Decepticons

Ending Status: It's complicated

Fun Fact: Switched sides after the Dark Cybertron event

G1 Cartoon

Origin: Created to lead the Decepticons into a new war

Ending Status: Became Galvatron and planned to keep conquering

Fun Fact: Was built by the Constructicons but later brainwashed them into serving him before creating them on Earth

Prime/WFC

Origin: Gladiator revolutionary who founded the Decepticons in his lust for power

Ending Status: Self-exiled after being freed from Unicron

Fun Fact: Used a corpses arm for some time

Animated

Origin: Overthrew the previous Decepticon leader

Ending Status: Imprisoned

Fun Fact: Spent years as a head

Earthspark

Origin: Unknown for now, probably the same or similar as G1

Ending Status: Ongoing

Fun Fact: His best friend is a human

Cyberverse

Origin: Former gladiator who was corrupted by a lust for power

Ending Status: Deceased

Fun Fact: Livestreamed Starscreams funeral after leaving him for dead
